When a restaurant is operating at full capacity, the **Front of House (FOH)** and **Back of House (BOH)** must work closely together. However, this coordination often leads to a lot of friction and confusion.

For example, common issues include:

- What exactly was the order?
- Where is the order right now?
- How many items were requested?
- How long will it take to prepare?

There can also be chaos on the floor—like a dish going missing with a waiter or orders getting mixed up.

This is where an POS Software comes in. It eliminates these frictions by streamlining communication and tracking. Think of it as a **lubricant for the entire restaurant system**, ensuring smooth, efficient operations even during peak hours.
